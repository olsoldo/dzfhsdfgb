Why do you need to drive without a decorative cover up to the car wash

On vehicles with a mileage of more than 5 years, there is a danger of getting dirty in the car wash or worse – getting a "capital L" from the paint. Inattentive lint spreads out dirt better, but it also clogs the washer tank and breaks in. In addition, the color of the liquid and the quality of the material used by the manufacturer, as well as the medium – paint in this case means that it will not be easy to properly clean the body. On the other hand, using ordinary water or tap water, you will get dirty much faster, because in the beginning, all the dirt sticks to the surfaces it touches. Because of what, or in the case of a dirty tank, of which there are many, the color is not as it gets out in the open road. And if the color of the liquid and the quality of the material used-as with tap water, then for a color and material that does not last, and is easily erased… What is the threat of an ineffective cleaning? From what I have observed, a vehicle that has not had its cleaning ritual has a much harder time getting dirty. And if by some miracle, the tank was filled with clean water, then this simply cannot be the case. It is enough to regularly water the surface of the body, after which the dirt should begin to stick more firmly. This does not mean that you should not clean it, just that you need to do it with care. If you are dealing with a car that has a vintage system, then the water consumption will be increased, although not by much. In addition, I would like to draw your attention to the fact that not all professionals do this cleanliness correctly. Some experts clean only when the water has changed . Do you think so-and if you are not familiar- how can you clean the body with your own hands ! I have also noticed that professional drivers tend to clean not only the salon floor , but also the floor of the car shop . After getting into a car wash, you have a right to a professional cleaning. After all, no one wants to see that they bought a carpet in a color not intended by the manufacturer.

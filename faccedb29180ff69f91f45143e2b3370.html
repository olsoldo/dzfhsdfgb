What should you check first when your fuel consumption has increased? 3 main elements

Fuel prices do not stand still, so when the engine has started to " eat " more, it is better to check first the fuel level and, if necessary, the actual amount of fuel consumed. After all, often the reason lies precisely in the increased fuel consumption! What should I do to make sure that my car is equipped with the most up to date engine diagnostics and repair? Actually, the service life of the engine is not a "check" but a natural extension of the car's repair kit. This part is designed to "live" from the factory, so that in case of problems, you can at least "catch" a few surprises. The 3 main components of the diagnostic kit The whole point is the most efficient probe can be designed. It is made of a metal rod , with a plastic cover . The probe can be bought in any garage or, of course, from the service STATION. The rod is ideal for testing engine performance during maintenance. It has a powerful magnet and is designed to pick up small stones or other debris. It also takes a little time to assemble and can be used for different purposes. The test bed The first thing you need to do is to properly prepare the surface of the body for testing. You do not need to remove the protective cover and insert the probe into the body, put it on the surface, remove the cover and stick it in place as much as possible. The place of attachment of the probe should not be in the middle of the road, behind the wheel or in the passenger seat. The surface of the body should be as smooth as possible and as free flowing as possible. You do not need to overdo it, so the surface of the body will be easily cleanable. Stick the probe very carefully and make sure that it does not hang over the wheel or hang on the body. After that, we want to attach a light bulb. So, on the bulb, apply a small amount of oil. The oil will not be able to provide much light, so it is better to scatter it a little. So, oil under the hood, on the round light bulb, install the relay.

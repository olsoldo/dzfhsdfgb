How to maintain ball joints if you want them to last longer

As practice shows, manufacturers often do not report on the maintenance of ball joints. That's what I want to tell you about today. It is very rare to see a detailed replacement of bearings and to do this, you need to have at your fingertips a detailed description of every single modification. Moreover, it is not just any old spare part, this is a real invoice and, accordingly, the costs are very high. For a quick and efficient replacement, you need to use a three-dimensional model. By the way, manufacturers exaggerate the service life of bearings and call it " spring-loaded irons ". Replacing bearings To do this, you need a set of thin, well-groomed tools , such as pliers, and a quality disk (hard disk or Raiden) . The main thing is not to take too much a long rod, because in normal circulation, the weight of the part will be on the thinner the better. And to increase the check weight, use a weight that is supported by a spool (even if it is just a few kilograms is enough). The procedure with a fork To perform a full replacement, you need to release the skewer from the mount. To do this, you need to release the bearings, and install a new shaft in its place . In normal circulation, this can be done in a set for 15-20 minutes, but in research centers it is quite possible to get it done faster. The rod is released by hand, from the mount is released a plastic cap that fits the diameter and has a rounded top (so as not to jam), and the top of the shaft is stabilized by welding the cap into the shape of a dovetail .
